Challenges of AI Art

AI Art has the potential to be unethical due to its data sourcing
methods and lack of informed consent. Its negative impact on
creatives has brought to light a discussion of Generative AI’s
greatest faults

Our Goal

We want to bring awareness to the negative
implications of AI Art and work to reform the use
of Artificial Intelligence in Artistry

Core Values:

  • Supporting Local Artists
  • Maintaining Credibility and Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Human Centered Technology

Call-to-Action:

  • Regulation of AI Tools
  • Re-evaluate how dataset for training are collected
  • Transparency regarding how data is used
  • Providing education on the ethics of AI and machine learning tools
  • Consent from the user
  • Citation of resources
